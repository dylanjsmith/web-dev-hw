Three Rules of Shooting Free Throws

  1. No matter how you shoot the ball, bend your knees
  2. Take a deep breath
  3. Aim for the middle hook on the back of the rim

How to Shoot a Free Throw

Think of yourself as a MACHINE

One MUST become consistent with their free throw routine to achieve rhythym. You have to dedicate yourself to shooting the ball the same way, every single time.

Relax, and JUST MAKE IT!

At the end of the day, what is most important is that you MAKE IT. Do whatever is necessary to make yourself comfortable so that you can make it.

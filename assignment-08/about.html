My inspiration for this project

I am an aspiring basketball coach, and I have worked with athletes ranging from preschool to professional. I hope to become a coach at a high level someday. I chose this project because I feel there could come a time when someone has to make a free throw to win a steak dinner and they could utilize my principles to lead them to victory. Most people don't realize the mental aspect that goes into being able to be a good free throw shooter. I just want my audience to know that simply relaxing and shooting the same way every single time increases the liklihood of succcess on an attempted shot. Shooting hoops is a great way to exercise and be in a competitive environment.
